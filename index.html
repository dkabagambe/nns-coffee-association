Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO) is committed to uplifting local communities through employment opportunities, health benefits, and economic growth. And we contribute to both social impact and economic growth by providing training to farmers, enhancing processing methods, and establishing local coffee shops.


Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO) is an organization that started in April 15, 2023 as a profitable and sustainable organization focused on transforming South Sudan’s coffee sector by enhancing coffee farming practices, establishing a reliable market for coffee beans, and expanding into regional and international markets. We produce, process and deliver high- quality organic coffee in areas of Nzara and Yambio with the local traditional farmers of excelsa who supply the organization with quality and pure organic excelsa coffee
