Find out more about the people behind Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO).


At Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO), we're dedicated to transforming South Sudan's coffee sector through sustainable practices and community empowerment. Our journey begins in Nzara and Yambio, where we partner with local Excelsa farmers to grow, process, and deliver premium, organic coffee. From farm to cup, we're committed to quality, social impact, and economic growth.
‍

Our story


Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO) was founded on April 15, 2023, in the heart of South Sudan's coffee-growing regions, Nzara and Yambio.
‍
We partner with local traditional Excelsa farmers, empowering their communities through sustainable practices and fair trade.
‍
Our mission is to enhance coffee farming practices, establish reliable markets, and expand into regional and international markets, reflecting our commitment to quality and excellence through the name CPDO Coffee.
‍
We take pride in managing the entire process of our coffee production, from nurturing local Excelsa coffee plants to processing, packaging, and distributing high-quality organic coffee beans, with onsite processing equipment.
‍
Our single-origin Excelsa coffee products can be found in selected retail outlets and our online store.

Our mission

Our mission at Coffee For Peace and Development Organization (CPDO) is to transform South Sudan's coffee sector. We strive to meet international and local market demand by establishing a reliable market for coffee beans and expanding into regional and international markets.

To achieve this, we collaborate with local coffee farmers, providing training, enhancing their coffee processing methods, and supporting them with coffee seedlings.

Through our partnership, we establish local coffee shops, ensuring a stable market for our high-quality, organic Excelsa coffee.

By empowering local farmers and promoting sustainable practices, we aim to establish South Sudan as a renowned producer of premium, organic coffee.

Our Vision

To establish South Sudan as a renowned producer of high-quality, organic coffee in local, regional, and international markets, fostering economic growth, sustainable farming, and improved health through organic coffee consumption.

Our Core Values

Quality: Commitment to delivering high-grade, organic coffee that meets international standards.

Sustainability: Emphasis on sustainable farming and processing practices that support the environment and local communities.

Community Empowerment: Focus on empowering local farmers, creating jobs, and enhancing economic opportunities.

Innovation: Dedication to improving traditional coffee processing techniques and embracing new technologies.

Health & Well-being: Promoting the health benefits of organic coffee for a healthier community.

Our Objectives

To deliver High-Quality Coffee Beans. Ensure a reliable supply of pure organic green coffee beans to meet the demand of local, regional, and international markets.

To bring Coffee Products to Local Communities. Provide the community with quality, final coffee products, including brewed coffee and finely processed coffee powder.

To enhance Health through Organic Coffee. Improve public health by promoting the consumption of high-quality, organic coffee with known health benefits.

Modernize Traditional Coffee Processing. Advance and innovate traditional methods of coffee processing to ensure high-quality output and greater marketability.

Our Key Activities

Farmer Training & Capacity Building.

Market Linkages & Export Promotion.

Establishment of Coffee Shops & Retail Outlets.

Improvement of Processing Techniques

Health Awareness Campaigns i.e. Educating the public on the benefits of organic coffee.

Production of other food crops.
