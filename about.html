At NNS Coffee Association, we're dedicated to transforming South Sudan's coffee sector through sustainable practices and community empowerment. Our journey begins in Nzara and Yambio, where we partner with local Excelsa farmers to grow, process, and deliver premium, organic coffee. From farm to cup, we're committed to quality, social impact, and economic growth.
‍

Our story


NNS Coffee Association was founded on April 15, 2023, in the heart of South Sudan's coffee-growing regions, Nzara and Yambio.
‍
We partner with local traditional Excelsa farmers, empowering their communities through sustainable practices and fair trade.
‍
Our mission is to enhance coffee farming practices, establish reliable markets, and expand into regional and international markets, reflecting our commitment to quality and excellence through the name NNS Coffee.
‍
We take pride in managing the entire process of our coffee production, from nurturing local Excelsa coffee plants to processing, packaging, and distributing high-quality organic coffee beans, with onsite processing equipment.
‍
Our single-origin Excelsa coffee products can be found in selected retail outlets and our online store.

Our Mission

Our mission at NNS Coffee Association is to transform South Sudan's coffee sector. We strive to meet international and local market demand by establishing a reliable market for coffee beans and expanding into regional and international markets.

To achieve this, we collaborate with local coffee farmers, providing training, enhancing their coffee processing methods, and supporting them with coffee seedlings.

Through our partnership, we establish local coffee shops, ensuring a stable market for our high-quality, organic Excelsa coffee.

By empowering local farmers and promoting sustainable practices, we aim to establish South Sudan as a renowned producer of premium, organic coffee.
